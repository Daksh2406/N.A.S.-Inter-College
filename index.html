Nanakchand Anglo Sanskrit College was established in the year 1952 by Nanakchand Trust, which was founded in 1903 in revered memory of Pandit Nanakchand — a sage and visionary, gifted with a rare combination of Wisdom, Wealth, and Will to strive for nation-building. The very name of the institution and the emblem — with Satyam Shivam Sundaram — is deeply rooted in the Indian Philosophy endeavoring to follow a positive growth in all possible directions.
